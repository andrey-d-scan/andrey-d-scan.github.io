3D projects

This project was created based on the drawings of the architectural studio Lighthouse projects, architects Tatyana and Dmitry Khoroshev. The following programs were used: AutoCAD and 3DsMax to create a 3D model, as well as Twinmotion for the final visualization.
The castle was made in 3Ds Max based on the original parts from Shenmue 3. In the game, the castle appears for a very short time and there is no way to look around it. In the video, you can discover new details that you did not see in the game
